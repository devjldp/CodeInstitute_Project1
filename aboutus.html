The Company

At Wedding Wishes & Dreams, we believe that your wedding day should be exactly as you've always dreamed it, and we're here to make that happen. We take a fresh approach to wedding planning, working closely with each client to design a personalized wedding that breaks the mold.

Our team of experienced wedding planners and designers are passionate about what they do, and it shows in every wedding we create. We believe that every wedding should be as unique as the couple getting married, and we're dedicated to making that a reality.

The Team!

Together, Sarah and Emma make a formidable team, dedicated to creating weddings that are as unique and unforgettable as the couples they work with. With their expertise and passion for wedding planning and design, they ensure that every wedding they plan is an experience that will be cherished for a lifetime.

Sarah, Founder and Lead Planner

Sarah is a seasoned wedding planner with over 10 years of experience in the industry. She has helped countless couples create one-of-a-kind weddings that perfectly capture their love story. Sarah is a master at coordinating every aspect of a wedding, from choosing the perfect venue to selecting the menu and music.

Emma, Event Designer and Planner

Emma is a creative and talented event designer with a passion for creating elegant and unique weddings. With over 7 years of experience in the wedding industry, she has worked with some of Cardiff's most renowned wedding companies. Emma specializes in designing and decorating wedding spaces that perfectly reflect the couple's style and personality.
